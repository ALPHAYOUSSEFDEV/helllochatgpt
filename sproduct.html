Product Details

Corsair 3000D The Corsair 3000D Airflow is a mid-tower ATX case designed for exceptional airflow and cooling potential. Its steel front panel and two pre-installed CORSAIR SP120 ELITE fans ensure optimal air circulation throughout the case, while the tempered glass side panel provides a clear view of your components. The intelligently designed interior can accommodate up to eight 120mm fans or four 140mm fans. With space for radiators up to 360mm, support for both 3.5" and 2.5" storage drives, and room for a four-slot graphics card up to 360mm in length, you can build a powerful high-end system. For convenience, the front panel includes two USB 3.2 Type-A ports and a combined microphone/audio jack.
